Web Design

These are two websites I have designed by myself. All coding and debugging was done by hand, and live links to each website can be found in the image captions.

Morris Benton Book Design

This is a book on the history and works of Morris Fuller Benton. It includes chapters on many aspects of his life and designs. The front cover has a pocket on the inside to hold three posters which each feature one of his most successful typefaces. The book is inspired by art deco architecture and design, and the patterns included are created out of letter-forms.

Logo and Typography Design

This is a collection of hand drawn typography and logos I have created.

PSFS Architecture Magazine

This is a fictional news letter that was created to highlight the features of the Philadelphia Savings Fund Society building. Graphic Design from the Bauhaus was used as inspiration for the layouts and covers.

The Shortfalls of Meat

The goal of this project was to create a museum wall that was part of a larger exhibit. I chose to discuss the dangers the cattle industry has on the environment. Infographics were incorporated to visually explain the dangers the cattle industry had on the environment. A scale model was built to show the dimensionality of the wall and its physical presence.

Philadelphia Union Media Guide

This is the official media Guide for the Philadelphia Union of Major League Soccer in 2011. The final copy was saved as a PDF and distributed to media personnel on USB thumb drives. A page from various sections were selected to demonstrate the layout as well as data organiation. Click Here for the full version

Tea Packaging

The objective of this project was to create three variations of tea packaging under one brand. All packages were required to knockdown flat while empty, and hold their structure by themselves. Vintage scientific illustrations were used to create designs that referenced Victorian era designs. Each tea emphasized the health benefits of specific herbal teas.
